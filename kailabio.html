“Hello Everyone!

My name is Kaila Peterson, I was born and raised in Chicago, IL in May 2, 1992. I currently reside in Chicago with my two lovely children Kareem Peterson and Olivia Peterson. I grew up in the Austin neighborhood of Chicago and attended Charles Sumner elementary and Ella Flagg Young elementary.

Once I received my eighth grade diploma, I was determined to enrolled into Providence St. Mel, a private school in the Garfield neighborhood. During my transition to high school, I struggled with lack of discipline and personal tribulations.

Years later, I came to a fork in the road of whether deciding to continue pursuing my higher education or give all of my time to the workforce, so my kids could have a wonderful future. Coincidently, my brother told me about the program called Year Up, a place with many opportunities to pursue. Even though, there was a rocky road leading up to this point, I am proud to say I’m currently attending Year Up and looking forward to graduation in 01/2017. ” ― Kaila Peterson
